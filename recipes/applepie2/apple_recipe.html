Apple Pie


Ingredients


  • 2 1/4 lbs Granny Smith Apples, peeled, cored, and thinly sliced (7 cups)
  • 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 8 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 Tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten with 1 Tbsp water, for egg wash
  • 1 recipe for double pie crust (Refer to specific recipe)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pie crust as per the recipe and chill while preparing the filling. Preheat oven to 425˚F.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour and simmer for 1 minute. Add water and sugar, bring to a boil, then simmer for 3 minutes.
  3. Place sliced apples in a large bowl, sprinkle with cinnamon, and coat with the sauce.
  4. Roll out the bottom crust to a 12" circle and transfer to a 9" pie plate. Add apple mixture.
  5. Roll out the second crust, cut into strips, and create a lattice over the filling. Brush with egg wash.
  6. Bake at 425˚F for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350˚F and bake for another 45 minutes.
  7. Let the pie rest at room temperature for 1 hour before serving.
